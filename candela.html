OVERVIEW

Candela explores how warmth can be designed with precision: soft light behavior, clear proportions and an honest material palette. The goal is a family system that can grow within esPattio’s universe without losing identity.

Design intent

Build a recognizable silhouette and a consistent detail language that supports multiple typologies. The emphasis is on a calm, Mediterranean presence that feels contemporary rather than nostalgic.

What I’m refining

Detail hierarchy (edges, joints, fixings), CMF strategy, and how the module scales across sizes. I’m also aligning decisions with realistic manufacturing constraints and assembly logic.

Next steps

Prototype key parts, validate light diffusion and assembly, and finalize the presentation set: family overview, detail shots, and in-context renders aligned with esPattio.
